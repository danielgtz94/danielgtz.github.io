How is the “Qualify %” calculated?
Every refresh runs a Monte Carlo simulation of the tournament. All matches already played are taken as final; each remaining group match is simulated 10,000 times by drawing goals from a Poisson model based on each team's scoring and conceding rate so far (regularised toward the tournament average so teams with few games aren't over-rated). For every simulated tournament the final group tables are computed (points, then goal difference, then goals for), the third-placed teams across all groups are ranked, and the best eight are taken. A team's Qualify % is the share of simulations in which it reaches the Round of 32 — so clinched teams trend to 100% and eliminated teams to 0% on their own. In-progress matches are re-simulated from the current scoreline as reported by the feed.
